Rosetta store: results across software

Comparisons with other software: Here you can find comparisons of results obtained in jamovi PAMLj, and other software, usually jamovi (jmv), pure R, and SPSS. The pages are primarily meant to test convergence between PAMLj and well-established packages, but they can be useful also to check how to get equivalent results across different programs.
